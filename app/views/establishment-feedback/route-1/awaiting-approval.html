User feedback

Participants are fine with waiting as they understand the care sector must be regulated. However, the non-user suggested this verification should have occurred after creating an account and not after committing time to add an establishment.

Participants would want an email verfication to confirm that their approval is in progress.

This procedure would disrupt their workflow. They would have set aside time to input NMDS-SC data and would have to reschedule - this may not be for several weeks.

1 working day waiting time was fine with users, but they would have preferred to have known earlier and would have scheduled their work to accommodate the delay.
