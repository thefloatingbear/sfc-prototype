What is NMDS-SC?

The National Minimum Data Set for Social Care (NMDS-SC) is an online workforce data collection system for the social care sector. Itâ€™s the leading source of robust workforce intelligence for adult social care.

It provides information on the size and structure of the whole adult social care sector.

Who should use it?

Users of NMDS-SC include:

  • adult social care providers
  • government departments, strategic bodies and policy makers
  • local authorities

They use it for a variety of reasons, including tracking staff retention rates, workforce modelling, policy making and planning resources.
